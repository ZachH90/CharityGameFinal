# Charity Water Impact Game

An interactive browser game inspired by real clean-water impact stories from Charity: Water.

Players complete three levels that represent different water-access challenges:
- Level 1: Rural Nepal community support
- Level 2: Rural Mali communities in the Sahel
- Level 3: Global-scale clean water impact

As you land water on the target, you build toward each level's gallon goal and unlock story screens that highlight the human impact behind the project.

## Gameplay Overview

- Aim and launch water to hit each level's community target.
- Fill each level's water goal to advance.
- Level 3 increases difficulty with a smaller moving target that relocates around the map after successful hits.
- Finish the final goal to unlock the global impact congratulations page and link out to Charity: Water.

## Controls

### Desktop

- Press `Space` once to lock arrow direction.
- Press `Space` again to lock power and throw water.
- After the throw completes, press `Space` to reset for the next attempt.

### Mobile / Touch

- Tap once to lock arrow direction.
- Tap again to lock power and throw water.
- Tap after the throw completes to reset for the next attempt.

## Audio Features

- Background music during gameplay
- Sound effects for throw, hit, miss, and win moments
- Audio controls in-game:
	- Sound toggle (On/Off)
	- Music volume slider
	- SFX volume slider

## Running the Game

This project is a static HTML/CSS/JS game.

1. Open [index.html](index.html) in a browser.
2. Click the splash screen to start.
3. Follow on-screen prompts through the story and levels.
